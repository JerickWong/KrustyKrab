Mr. Eugene Harold Krabs

The proud founder and owner of the most popular restaurant in Bikini Bottom, Mr. Eugene Krabs--affectionately called Mr. Krabs by his friends and family--is a world-class businessman whose personal logo is: "I love money."

With a passion for money and a little bit of business savviness, Eugene started his fast food business early in his childhood with former best friend and current business competitor, Sheldon J. Plankton. The Krabby Patty formula was created during one of Mr Krabs and Plankton's struggles. After that, it was smooth sailing.

The Krabby Patty formula and Eugene's money-grabbing skills, along with trusty sidekicks Spongebob Squarepands and Squidward Tentacles, helped propel the fledgling Krusty Krabs into popularity, eventually becoming the most well-known restaurant in Bikini Bottom.
